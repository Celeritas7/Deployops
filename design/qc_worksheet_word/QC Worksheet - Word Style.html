Pre-install QC
# Item Notes / Description OP QC
1
Visual inspection of pillar.
 Check for cracks, dents and corrosion on visible welds. No tools needed.
2
Apply primer to base plate.
Primer mark applied to bolt heads after priming.
3
Tighten 16 base bolts to 80 Nm.
Torque mark applied on each bolt head after tightening.
4
Verify GraboC coupling orientation.
STOP. Supervisor review required before proceeding.
 Check the arrow on the coupling housing points toward the front of the pillar. N/A SUP
5
Secure pillar in forklift cradle.
STOP. Witness required. Do not proceed alone.
 Cradle straps must be locked on both sides before any bolts come off. N/A WIT
6
Forklift operator in position.
STOP. Witness required. Do not proceed alone.
 Forklift operator at the controls with hands on the wheel before the next step. N/A WIT
7
Remove 16 pillar mounting bolts.
STOP. Witness required. Do not proceed alone.
 Remove diagonally opposite bolts in pairs. Last two only after the cradle is taking load. N/A WIT
8
Lower pillar into transit cradle.
STOP. Witness required. Do not proceed alone.
 Lower slowly. Pillar must rest fully on the transit cradle before straps are released. N/A WIT
Pre-test QC
# Item Notes / Description OP QC
1
Power-on self test.
 Confirm all status LEDs go green within 30 seconds of power on.
2
Calibrate base joint encoder.
Calibration log saved to USB and unit serial recorded on log.
3
Run end-effector home routine.
Home position within 0.2 mm of nominal on all axes.
4
Verify e-stop cuts power within 200 ms.
STOP. Supervisor review required before proceeding.
 Press e-stop while arm is in slow-jog. Supervisor reads timer trace before clearing. N/A SUP
5
Run full-payload lift test (200 kg).
STOP. Witness required. Do not proceed alone.
 Clear test cell of personnel. Witness watches from outside the cell, monitors load cell reading. N/A WIT
6
Hold-and-release test at full reach.
STOP. Witness required. Do not proceed alone.
 Hold load for 60 s at full horizontal reach. Witness confirms no drift on dial gauge. N/A WIT
